Project Mandolin is an initiative to disrupt the existing structure of government and international politics. The concentration of power with policy makers and their lack of agency in issues is a seriously flawed structure. A top down approach of represention to coalesece the beliefs and desires of a diverse range of people into a single policy is a noble but unrealistic objective. The optimisation objective of producing the best policy for a varying range of views is inherently flawed

The UN has been historically hampered in intervening in armed conflicts due to its need to remain neutral and its dependency on member states to contribute armed forces. Notable examples of this were in Rwanda and Bosnia. We believe the internet is a powerful platform to enable private citizens to take action against war crimes at an early stage, before policy makers decide to act

FAQ

What is Project:Mandolin?
An initiative to empower individuals to engage in interational diplomacy.
Why are we doing this?
The traditional top down model of representation is flawed in its attempt to find the optimal solution for a diverse set of individuals. We believe the internet is an enabler of a new model of responsive citizenship and international diplomacy.
